$$ P(\text{Match}) = 0.98 \cdot \prod_{g \in \{M,F\}} \left[ \frac{\prod_{i=0}^{b_g-1} (A_{g, \text{pool}} \cdot \mathbb{I}_{A}(i) + (N_{g, \text{pool}} - A_{g, \text{pool}}) \cdot (1 - \mathbb{I}_{A}(i)) - i)}{\prod_{i=0}^{b_g-1} (N_{g, \text{pool}} - i)} \cdot \binom{\text{freie}_g}{b_g} \right] \cdot \prod_{k=1}^5 \mathcal{H}(\sum \text{aktiv}_k \le 10) $$
